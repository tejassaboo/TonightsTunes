Discover New Artists

Escape from your comfort zone. Challenge yourself to connect with new artists, discover a new favorite song, and enjoy live performances with friends and family. Our matching algorithm will help you find music that matches your preferences--simply select your favorite genres, the maximum distance that you are willing to travel, your ideal event size, dates and times that you are free, and your price range.

Help Local Artists Grow!

Support local artists in their creative pursuits by inviting your friends and family to explore new music at their casual concerts. Don't forget to purchase their merchandise to remind you of a fond memory--then snap a photo and share on social media to continue helping local artists get discovered.

Accelerate An Artist's Adaption Process

The creative process of an artist can be significantly improved with feedback. Please provide insights, constructive criticisms, appreciations, and other comments regarding an artist's performance, music/beat/lyrics, style, casual concert execution, merchandise, and anything else that you think could help. The goal is to help local artists learn from feedback to improve future performances.
